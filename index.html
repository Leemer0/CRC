About The Club

Modern indoor tennis in Maryhill.

Who We Are

Who we are

Located in Maryhill at the heart of Waterloo Region, our tennis club offers easy access for players from Waterloo, Kitchener, Guelph and Cambridge. We are proud to be a welcoming home for tennis enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels from beginners picking up a racquet for the first time to competitive players looking to sharpen their game. With access, top-quality courts, and a friendly community, our club is a place where great tennis and great connections come together on your schedule.

What We Offer

Membership Benefits

Members enjoy priority access and exclusive perks designed to elevate their tennis experience both on and off the court.

  • Priority Court Booking with a 7-day advance booking window, giving members first access to prime court times
    (Public and non-members are limited to a 3-day booking window)
  • Two complimentary guest passes per month, perfect for inviting friends or family to play
  • Access to professional coaching, including private lessons and structured training opportunities
  • Discounted court fees available exclusively to members
  • Eligibility for club-run tournaments and ladder leagues, fostering competitive play and community engagement
  • Access to tennis clinics and seasonal programs, including youth programs and summer camps
  • Extended daily court access, offering flexibility to play around your schedule
  • And more exclusive benefits added throughout the year

Membership is designed to provide flexibility, value, and a connected club experience, whether you are training competitively or playing socially.

Location

Easy access across Waterloo Region.

Our indoor tennis facility is located at 1303 Maryhill Road in Maryhill, offering convenient access for players across the Waterloo Region. We are just 10 minutes from Guelph, 15 minutes from Waterloo and Kitchener, and 25 minutes from Cambridge, making us an ideal choice for anyone searching for indoor tennis courts near Waterloo, Guelph, Kitchener, or Cambridge . Designed for year-round play, our modern courts provide a comfortable environment for players of all levels looking for reliable, high-quality indoor tennis close to home.
